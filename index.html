Tatsam Anand (born 20 June 2009), known professionally as MrJhola, is an Indian guitarist and independent solo musician from Patna, Bihar. He specializes in guitar-driven music influenced by rock and metal genres.

Early Life

Anand was born in Patna, Bihar, to Ratna Shree and Anand Prakash.

Career

As a solo artist, he creates and releases original compositions independently across global digital streaming platforms. His work is characterized by technical proficiency and a focus on rock and metal soundscapes.
